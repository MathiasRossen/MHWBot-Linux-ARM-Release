Use this command to start a challenge.
Usage: !addchallenge "Challenge name" weaponId

"Challenge name" - Specify a challege name within the quote symbols.

weaponId - Used to determine which weapon should be used for the challenge.
0 = Sword and Shield
1 = Dual Blades
2 = Long Sword
3 = Great Sword
4 = Hammer
5 = Hunting Horn
6 = Switch Axe
7 = Charge Blade
8 = Lance
9 = Gunlance
10 = Light Bowgun
11 = Heavy Bowgun
12 = Insect Glaive
13 = Bow
